    • Location

    Vila Vanda is located in Rtina, a quiet coastal village in Croatia, part of the beautiful Zadar region. The property is surrounded by clear blue sea, peaceful nature, and traditional Dalmatian charm. Its location offers the perfect balance between relaxation and convenience — with the city of Zadar and many stunning beaches, islands, and attractions just a short drive away. Whether you want to enjoy calm days by the sea or explore the surrounding area, Vila Vanda is the ideal starting point for your holiday.
