    Vanda

    Apartment Vanda is our largest accommodation, located on the ground floor of Vila Vanda. It offers a spacious and comfortable environment, perfect for up to four guests. With its convenient layout and easy access, it’s an ideal choice for families or small groups looking to enjoy a relaxing stay by the sea.

    Mila

    Apartment Mila is located on the upper floor of Vila Vanda and is perfect for up to four guests. It offers a cozy and comfortable space with a private balcony, ideal for relaxing and enjoying the peaceful surroundings. With its bright atmosphere and quiet setting, it’s a perfect choice for couples, solo travelers or families.

    Nina

    Apartment Nina is located on the upper floor of Vila Vanda and can accommodate up to four guests. It features a lovely balcony, perfect for enjoying the sea view, fresh air, and unforgettable sunsets. The apartment offers a bright and comfortable atmosphere, ideal for a relaxing stay.
